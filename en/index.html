Reform

Making the Google Forms pre-fill feature useful

Sample URL

About

Google Forms can pass parameters externally through the pre-fill feature.
To take advantage of this feature, you need to look up the field IDs, and this tool automatically extracts the field IDs and converts them into generic HTML.
You can use the field IDs to dynamically generate URLs for federations and place forms of any design you want.

How to use

Paste the URL of the Google Forms and then press the "Reform".
